Four threads

The things I keep coming back to.

A mixed bag of obsessions — half technical, half tactile.

01 Cosmos
Space & the long now

I think space is the closest thing we have to a free perspective upgrade. A single photograph of Earth from low orbit is enough to recalibrate a bad week.

I follow space missions the way some people follow football — Artemis, the JWST archive, and the slow drumbeat of private launches.

02 Tech
Gadgets & quiet engineering

The right tool, well used. I'm interested in how everyday devices actually work and how the boring infrastructure behind them holds up.

I like practical technology more than hype, especially when it solves real problems cleanly.

03 Travel
Travel as a refresh

New cities, new neighbourhoods, a working SIM card and a notebook — travel keeps my perspective from getting stale.

It gives me a reset and a different angle on routines, work and everyday life.

04 Kitchen
Cooking, slowly

Food is a small daily ritual for me — Brazilian food, weeknight pastas, and the occasional ambitious Sunday project.

Cooking is one of the easiest ways to slow down and make something worth sharing.
